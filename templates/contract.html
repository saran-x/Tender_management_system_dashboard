Contract Rules & Regulations

Standard Terms and Conditions for Tender Contracts

Important Notice

This document outlines the standard rules and regulations governing all tender contracts. Please read carefully before proceeding with any contractual agreements.

General Terms & Conditions

1
Contract Validity

All contracts shall remain valid for the duration specified in the tender document. Extensions may be granted upon mutual agreement.

2
Payment Terms

Payments shall be made according to the schedule outlined in the contract. Late payments may incur penalties as specified.

3
Delivery Timeline

Vendors must adhere to the delivery timeline specified in the contract. Delays must be communicated in writing with valid reasons.

Vendor Obligations

4
Quality Standards

All deliverables must meet the quality standards specified in the tender document. Substandard work will be rejected.

5
Documentation

Vendors must maintain proper documentation for all transactions and deliverables as per regulatory requirements.

6
Compliance

Vendors must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards throughout the contract period.

Client Rights & Responsibilities

7
Inspection Rights

The client reserves the right to inspect work progress and quality at any stage of the project.

8
Termination Rights

The client may terminate the contract for valid reasons including consistent underperformance or breach of terms.

9
Modification Rights

The client may request reasonable modifications to the scope of work, subject to mutual agreement and price adjustment.

Financial Terms

10
Bid Bond

A bid bond may be required as security for serious bid submission. This amount is refundable upon contract completion.

11
Performance Bond

A performance bond may be required to ensure contract fulfillment. This is typically 5-10% of the contract value.

12
Payment Schedule

Payments will be released according to the agreed schedule upon satisfactory completion of milestones.

Legal Provisions

13
Dispute Resolution

All disputes shall be resolved through arbitration before pursuing legal action. The arbitration venue shall be mutually agreed upon.

14
Governing Law

This contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where the client is based.

15
Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during the contract period.

Acceptance of Terms

By proceeding with the tender process, vendors acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these rules and regulations.
